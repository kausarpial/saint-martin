Since 1999 . . .

Saint Martin Group is Bangladesh’s one of the prominent corporate group with diversified concentrations in various sectors such as, Food, Dairy, Commodity, Information Technology (IT), Logistics and Transportation, Infrastructure Development etc.

Saint Martin Group has revolutionized the frozen food sector in Bangladesh and abroad. The Group’s development over the last 20 years has been a trial-blazer on the Bangladeshi horizon. Bangladesh being a developing third world nation marked by lush greenery and many waterways, this spells nothing short of very hard-earned success.

Saint Martin Seafood
